Privacy Policy Calseva

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

CalSEVA ("us", "we", or "our") operates the CalSEVA mobile and web application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal and operational data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our core privacy philosophy is simple: Your data belongs to you.

  • Local Storage: All asset details, machinery specifications, QR codes, scheduling calendars, and generated calibration reports are stored strictly in your device's internal local storage.
  • No Server Uploads: We do not collect, transmit, or sync your calibration logs or machinery records to any remote, third-party, or cloud-based database servers.

2. Permissions Requested

To provide full functionality, CalSEVA may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Storage/Files Access: Required to save, export, and download generated PDF calibration reports and QR code images directly to your device.
  • Camera Access (Optional): Required if you utilize the app's built-in camera utility to scan asset QR codes for quick equipment lookup.

3. Third-Party Services

The application may utilize local device libraries to render files (such as local PDF generation engines). These processes happen entirely on-device. If we utilize basic analytics to track application crashes or performance metrics, no personally identifiable information (PII) or industrial asset details are ever captured.

4. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your mobile device or web browser cache, the security of your data depends entirely on your device’s security settings. We highly recommend using device-level encryption, secure lock screens, and performing manual backups of exported reports.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy directly within the application interface. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
