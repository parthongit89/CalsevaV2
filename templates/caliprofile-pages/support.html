Quick Troubleshooting FAQ's

Where is my calibration data stored? expand_more

Your data is stored 100% locally on your device's internal storage (or browser cache for web). CalSEVA does not use cloud servers to host your asset logs, which keeps your industrial data entirely private and secure.

I accidentally uninstalled the app. Can I recover my schedules? expand_more

Because data is kept strictly on your mobile device, deleting the app or clearing its storage data wipes the local database. We cannot recover this data for you. This is why we highly recommend using the Export Data/PDF feature at the end of every shift.

The PDF Report is not downloading. What should I do? expand_more

Check if you have granted CalSEVA Storage/Files permission in your device settings. Ensure your device has at least 50 MB of free internal storage. If using the web version, check if your browser blocked a pop-up window or download prompt.
